Our Menu

CozyCupCaffe, we offer a delightful selection of freshly brewed coffee and delicious pastries. From rich, aromatic espresso to buttery croissants and muffins, our menu is crafted to bring comfort and satisfaction to every visit. Whether you're grabbing a quick morning pick-me-up or enjoying a relaxing afternoon treat, CozyCupCaffe is the perfect spot to indulge in quality flavors and cozy vibes.

Cappuccino

A cappuccino is an espresso-based drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, known for its rich texture and strong flavor.

Latte

A latte is an espresso-based drink made with espresso and steamed milk, topped with foam, offering a smooth, creamy flavor.

Espresso

An espresso is a strong, concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans, known for its bold, rich flavor.

Macchiato

A macchiato is an espresso-based drink with a small amount of steamed milk or foam, offering a strong coffee flavor with a hint of creaminess.

Croissants

A croissant is a buttery, flaky pastry with a crescent shape, known for its light layers and rich flavor.

Muffins

A muffin is a soft, baked treat with a moist, fluffy texture, often flavored with fruits, nuts, or chocolate, and served in a cup-like shape.

Scones

A muffin is a soft, baked treat with a moist, fluffy texture, often flavored with fruits, nuts, or chocolate, and served in a cup-like shape.

Tarts

A **tart** is a baked dessert with a pastry crust and a sweet or savory filling, often topped with fruits, custard, or cream.
